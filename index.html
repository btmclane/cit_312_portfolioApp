Davis Love III (476 Yards) Davis Love III recorded the PGA Tour's longest drive ever recorded, in the ShotlLink era, at the 2004 Mercedes Championship.

Craig Barlow (111ft. 5in.) Craig Barlow drained the longest putt ever recorded, in the ShotLink era, at the 2008 Buick Open for an eagle.

Andrew Magee (332 Yards) Andrew Magee recorded the longest hole in one ever measured at TPC Scottsdale in the first round of the 2001 Phoenix Open.
